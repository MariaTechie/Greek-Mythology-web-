Aphrodite: Goddess of Love and Beauty In Greek Mythology

Mystery In History
Visiter le site
Aphrodite is the Greek goddess of love, beauty, and desire. Born from sea foam, she embodies the creative forces of nature. Her irresistible charm captivates both gods and mortals. Notably, she played a role in sparking the Trojan War by promising Helen to Paris. Symbols like roses, doves, and swans reflect the love and beauty she represents.
