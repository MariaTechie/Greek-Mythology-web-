The Land of Gods and Monsters is an online resource on Greek mythology, featuring profiles of deities and creatures with encyclopedia summaries, quotes from ancient texts, and illustrations from ancient art. The site uses transliterated Greek names (e.g., Kirke instead of Circe), with Latin names on the index and introduction pages for reference. It also includes a collection of classical literature, summaries of major gods and creatures, and a family tree of the Greek pantheon. Other features include maps of mythological kingdoms, themed pages on plants, flowers, and star myths, and a gallery of over 1200 ancient artworks. The site is searchable via a Google box.
